Traktable

A Trakt.tv scrobbler for iTunes and AppleTV.

About

The app scrobbles all your played movies and tv episodes from iTunes or Apple TV to Trakt.tv. New since version 0.5 is the scrobble queue where failed scrobbles are saved for a retry.
